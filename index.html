Self-Imposed Blobs


This animation is a product of my reflections on my experience with anxiety. I thought that my anxiety was a result of external factors, the people and event happening around me. If I cut these things out of my life then my problems would be solved, right? It took a while to realize that the cause of my anxiety was my own perception of the things around me. I can't get rid of it if the source is me. It takes a lot of self-awareness to take a step back and consider if the way I am percieving things in a positive way.
